ACE: Athlete Capital Exchange

A Decentralized Protocol for Tokenized Athlete Income Streams (TAIS)

Version 2.0 | November 2025

1. Executive Summary

The introduction of Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL) rights has created a new asset class: the future earning potential of student-athletes. However, the current financial infrastructure is fragmented and predatory. ACE creates a "Value-Extraction & Acceleration" model, transforming illiquid NIL contracts into productive, liquid capital through compliant smart contract architecture.

2. The Problem: Idle Capital

  • The Liquidity Gap: Athletes face a timing mismatch between signing deals and receiving payments, driving them to predatory lenders.
  • Exclusionary Economics: Fans and small donors are excluded from the financial upside of the ecosystem they support.
  • Financial Illiteracy: Sudden wealth is often mismanaged without embedded financial guardrails.

3. The Solution: TAIS Protocol

ACE introduces Tokenized Athlete Income Streams (TAIS). Verified contracts are tokenized and deposited into a Smart Contract Escrow. Athletes borrow stablecoins against this collateral. The "Set-Off" mechanism automatically routes endorsement payments to the contract for repayment before releasing funds to the athlete, eliminating behavioral default risk.

4. Risk Management & Governance

4.1 Transfer Portability: ACE protocols require standardized contracts with portability clauses. If an athlete transfers, the receiving institution's Collective must buyout the debt obligation.

4.2 Insurance Wrappers: A portion of protocol yield (2%) is diverted to an Insurance Pool to cover principal in events of career-ending injury or contract cancellation.

4.3 Regulatory Compliance: Participation is structured as DAO membership with rewards for active governance, distinguishing it from passive investment securities.

5. Conclusion

ACE is not just a loan; it is infrastructure. By applying institutional credit standards to sports finance, we solve the liquidity crisis while offering investors a secured, high-yield asset class rooted in real-world revenue.

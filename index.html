I'm currently building ACE (Athlete Capital Exchange) — a regulated FinTech protocol establishing the infrastructure for Productive Capital in sports. ACE shifts the paradigm from static NIL contracts to a model of Value Extraction & Acceleration, enabling athletes to unlock future earnings today to invest in their own human capital.

My professional background is the foundation for this initiative. With experience spanning Wealth Management at Merrill Lynch, Commercial Credit, and Major Gifts Fundraising for D1 athletics, I recognized a systemic failure: athletes possess valuable assets but lack the compliant financial tools to leverage them. ACE solves this liquidity gap.

Alongside this, I am developing a minimalist apparel line. This is the "Creative" to ACE's "Capital"—focusing on essential forms, premium materials, and limited runs that embody the brand's ethos of discipline and quality.

Prior to all of this, I was a Division I athlete at Clemson University. This unique blend of finance and high-performance sports informs a central theme in all my work: creating new opportunities for others to transform potential into power.

Value Extraction & Acceleration

The Concept: ACE creates a secure mechanism for asset liquidity. We enable capital to flow efficiently to high-potential athletes based on verifiable future income. This model allows for the immediate extraction of value from future contracts, accelerating the athlete's ability to invest in their own human capital and career development.

The Utility: This is a capital efficiency engine. Investors provide necessary working capital, secured by contract revenue. Athletes gain the resources to maximize their performance and market value. Yield is generated directly from the endorsement contracts, creating a clear, rigorous, and sustainable financial cycle.
